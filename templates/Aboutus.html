Founder

Founded in 2010 by Mr. Yogendra Kumar Jain and his son Lokesh Jain, our firm has become a cornerstone of the electronics and furniture industry. The journey of our enterprise is deeply rooted in the extensive experience and visionary leadership of Mr. Yogendra Kumar Jain, who brings over 30 years of experience in the retail business. His journey began in 1991 when he ventured into the hardware submersible business, demonstrating a keen understanding of market dynamics and a commitment to quality and innovation. Before embarking on his entrepreneurial path, Mr. Yogendra Kumar Jain honed his skills and gained valuable insights while working in a government office. This diverse background provided him with a unique perspective and a strong foundation in both administrative and operational aspects of business management. His transition from public service to the retail sector was marked by a relentless pursuit of excellence and a deep-seated passion for customer satisfaction. In 2010, recognizing the burgeoning potential in the electronics market, Mr. Yogendra Kumar Jain, alongside his son Lokesh Jain, decided to establish a firm that would offer cutting-edge electronics products to consumers. The father-son duo combined their strengths—Yogendra's vast experience and Lokesh's innovative approach—to create a business that quickly gained a reputation for reliability and superior customer service.
